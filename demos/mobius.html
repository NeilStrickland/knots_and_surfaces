Möbius strip

The Möbius strip is the simplest nonorientable surface. It can be parametrised as $$ (x,y,z) = (3\cos(\theta) + s\cos(\theta/2), 3\sin(\theta), s\sin(\theta/2)), $$ with $-1 \lt s\lt 1$. It is not orientable, and is also not closed.
